A foot rheostat is available for variable speed control.
To connect, join motor cord plug with rheostat plug.
See parts list.
Assembly instructions -- the base and column are assembled together at the factory, as are also the motor and table bracket assemblies.
Slide the table bracket assembly first on the column, position and tighten.
The motor assembly is then slid on the column.
Line up the motor with the table and tighten temporarily with hand wheel.
Insert drill into chuck and line up drill with hole in center of table.
